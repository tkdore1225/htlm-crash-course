The Fake History of Gaming

The Legend of Zelda

First we have Legend of Zelda. Marvelous game indeed, filled my childhood with many wonderful memories. It was around 2005 when i played my first Zelda game, and boy let me tell you. The music was made by an orchestra of angels. It was like God Himself was the composer. This music will strike you with the most heavenly feeling of nostalgia. Yes, you will not forget this game much less the music for as long as you live. Mainly because it is timeless (and it goes viral a lot). Im not kidding.

Pokemon Soul Silver

Now we have one of the OG pokemon games every created. So good that they remade it with better quality 2016. People liked it that much that that had to be done. My naivety got the best of in the original version since i was 10 but i dominated the remake, ensuring i captured each and every single strong legendary pokemon and destroyed RED. Nonetheless, very fun game indeed. Would buy again.

Kingdom Hearts

Last but certainly not least, Kingdom motherfreakin Hearts. Top tier game, similar to the other two. Supplies you with an ungodly amount of great freakin time. Song nostalgia? Check. Actually good storyline that you can relate to deeply in middle school but also in your adulthood? Check. This game has it all. It won't be dettered with you buying it because of the immense support it gets from it's undying fanbase. Good luck recreating a game like this again much less the other two.
